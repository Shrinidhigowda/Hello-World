Dog

Introduction:

  • A dog is a domestic mammal of the family Canidae and the order Carnivora.
  • Dogs are one of the two most ubiquitous and most popular domestic animals in the world.
  • Many people have learned that one human year equals roughly seven dog years.
  • At three months old, dogs can receive a rabies vaccine, which is readministered every year or every three years.
  • Barking is a way for dogs to communicate with other dogs and with humans.
  • However, dogs also bark when they are bored and want attention from other dogs or from humans.
  • The companionship between humans and dogs is not a new phenomenon.
  • The next consideration is the selection of a particular type of dog.
  • No matter what kind of dog a person chooses, it is essential that it be a healthy animal.
  • The dog should appear friendly and outgoing.
  • Puppies need three basic things in order to thrive: good nutrition, warmth, and companionship.
  • There should be steady growth on a weekly basis, but there should be no excess fat around the abdomen.
  • How a dog ages is based largely on its size and breed.
